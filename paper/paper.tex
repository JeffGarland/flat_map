\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:intro}

This paper outlines what a (mostly) API-compatible, non-node-based
\code{std::map} might look like.  Rather than presenting a final design, this
paper is intended as a starting point for discussion and as a basis for future
work.  Specifically, there is no mention of \code{multimap}, \code{set}, or
\code{multiset}.  Those will be added in later papers.
